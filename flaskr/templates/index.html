The Beer With Me application started in a small two bedroomed apartment in Limerick. After a long night of general debauchery, plenty of merriment and many bottles of bland tasteless beer, we decided enough was enough. Something had to be done. Life was too short to drink bad beer. The next morning, while nursing a spectacular hangover we decided to set out in making an application. That highlights the best of beer! There is a rich brewing history in Ireland and it is engrained in our DNA. We felt it was our job to spread the word.

We offer comprehensive information on pubs in Limerick. Find the best venues, deals and events wherever you are, whenever you want. Search for venues by name or just browse for pubs close by on the interactive map. Looking for something to do? Beer With Me provides quick access to pub content including food and drink specials, happy hours, special events, trivia & poker nights.

Who We Are


Our vision is to provide our USERS with the most comprehensive and useful pub app in the world. We strive to connect our VENUES with their customers by creating new and easy to use communication channels. Our EMPLOYEES are proud of the products and services we produce. We believe that pubs play an important role within local communities; a meeting place where social networks are strengthened and extended, as well as hosting a wide variety of community oriented events and activities that add considerably to local civic life.
