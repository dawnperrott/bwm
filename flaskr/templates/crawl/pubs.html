Treaty City

Treaty City Brewery started in a small two bedroomed apartment in East Vancouver Canada. After a long night of general debauchery, plenty of merriment and many bottles of bland tasteless beer, we decided enough was enough. Something had to be done. Life was too short to drink bad beer. The next morning, while nursing a spectacular hangover we headed for the local homebrew store and purchased our very first brewing equipment.

With the new addition to the family and our own Clan so far away we duly set our sights on the emerald Isle. With a quick hop and a skip we were back in the Treaty City and ready for action. Treaty City Brewery was incorporated, labels were created, designs and reviews of everything from bottle caps to t-shirts. Stephen set to work, diligently tasting as many beers as he could (It was very hard work) and building the recipes for the iconic beers that are on the shelves today.

Crew Brewing Co.

We brew our own beer on site at the back of the pub and serve it directly from tanks behind the bar. We believe that great beer is best enjoyed fresh and that nothing beats the experience of a flavourful beer direct from the tank at the brewery. Pop in on the right day and you might find the bar filled with the smell of malt and see our staff brewing through our brewery windows while you enjoy your pint. We produce a huge range of styles from the classics to the highly experimental brews, all in small batches with a focus on producing flavourful, quality, highly drinkable craft beer. All of our beers are original creations by our team led by Master Brewer Jono. Through the year we’ll produce over 50 different beers as well as repeating some of our and our customer’s favourites so feel free to let us know what you’d like to see us brew again!

We’re excited to share our love of fresh, local beer with you. We’re proud to be a small independent business, a living wage employer, a sustainability focused business and a positive contributor to our community.

The White House

Established in 1812 and situated in the professional and business heart of Limerick City the White House Bar is as famous for its connections with the local literary arts as it is for the heritage of the bar itself. Once the 10 year host of the White House Poets led by the larger than life and sadly missed Mr Barney Sheehan it is again beginning to reconnect with these roots whilst maintaining the high standards demanded of the traditional Irish pub. Simply put, the White House is where the art of conversation is held highest and “The Weather, Traffic & Sports” are debated in ways not often heard.
